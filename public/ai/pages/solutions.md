# Solutions Page Narrative

Canonical page: https://devrecon.in/solutions

The Solutions page explains the main categories of work DevReCon can help with. It is designed to avoid fixed packages and instead route people by the nature of their technical problem.

## AI Consultation and Applications

DevReCon helps define where AI belongs, what data it needs, and whether the path should use an LLM workflow, RAG pipeline, automation layer, product feature, or custom model.

Useful when:

- The AI use case is unclear.
- A team needs an assistant, automation flow, document workflow, or AI-powered feature.
- A generic API may not be enough.

## Custom Software Development

DevReCon studies the workflow first, then shapes the product structure, interface, data flow, and development path around the people who use the system.

Useful when:

- A process still runs through spreadsheets, manual coordination, or disconnected tools.
- A portal, dashboard, internal platform, or SaaS-style foundation is needed.
- A product needs clear architecture before expansion.

## Frontier Tech and IoT

DevReCon maps physical systems and digital layers together so devices, data, dashboards, and user experience do not become separate problems.

Useful when:

- The product involves sensors, connected hardware, physical interaction, or field conditions.
- A prototype needs software logic, dashboard access, or feasibility direction.
- Physical and digital sides need planning before resources are committed.

## Technical Strategy and Deployment

DevReCon helps teams decide sequence, architecture, risk, hosting, maintenance, documentation, and what should be built first.

Useful when:

- The idea is strong but the technical path is unclear.
- A product needs architecture, roadmap, deployment direction, or documentation.
- An existing system needs review before expansion, rebuild, or handover.

## Inquiry Guidance

Send inquiries to management@devrecon.in. Include context, current stage, expected outcome, timeline if any, and any existing tools, users, data, or constraints.
