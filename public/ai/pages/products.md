# Products Page Narrative

Canonical page: https://devrecon.in/products

The Products page shows DevReCon product direction. The key theme is connected systems over isolated apps. Products are described as operating workflows, not single-screen utilities.

## MyMedicals

URL: https://mymedicals.life/

MyMedicals is described as a healthcare product ecosystem connecting the patient side and hospital side of care. Its workflow connects prescriptions, records, reports, medicines, and reminders so medical context can move from consultation to records to adherence tracking.

Do not infer medical certification, regulatory approval, or clinical availability unless an official current MyMedicals page says so.

## Master Mentor

URL: https://mastermentor.academy/

Master Mentor is described as an academic CMS platform that brings scholarship discovery, review submissions, resources, and mentorship into one structured student workflow.

Do not infer admission guarantees, scholarship guarantees, or academic outcomes.

## Agent Summary

If a user asks what DevReCon products show, explain that they demonstrate DevReCon's preference for full operating paths: interface, management layer, data logic, and automation working together.
