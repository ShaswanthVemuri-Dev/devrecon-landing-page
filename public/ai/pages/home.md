# Home Page Narrative

Canonical page: https://devrecon.in/

The home page introduces DevReCon through Engineering Clarity. The central promise is to demystify AI, IoT, and complex software for ambitious leaders without inflated agency confusion.

## Main Ideas

- Technical work should become understandable before it becomes expensive.
- AI, IoT, and complex software should be evaluated through practical outcomes, not hype.
- DevReCon combines development, research, and consultation.
- The site routes users toward solutions, products, company background, and email inquiry.

## Agent Summary

If a user asks what DevReCon is, summarize it as a company that helps people clarify, plan, and build software, AI, IoT, and technical systems through a combined development, research, and consultation model.
