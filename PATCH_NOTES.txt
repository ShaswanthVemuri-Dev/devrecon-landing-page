DevReCon stabilization and discoverability notes

Current status:
- Mobile navigation was rebuilt as a retained shell state machine so the navbar expands and closes predictably on phones.
- Scroll reveal behavior was consolidated around a single once-only reveal pattern without phone/iPad/desktop branching.
- Solutions accordion behavior was stabilized with deterministic height transitions and simpler mobile layout.
- Product preview hover/tap behavior was intentionally preserved and not refactored.
- Hostinger SPA fallback is handled through public/.htaccess so BrowserRouter direct routes can resolve to index.html.
- Public crawler and AI guidance files were added: robots.txt, sitemap.xml, llms.txt, llms-full.txt, agents.txt, and AI narrative documents under public/ai.
- Structured data was added to index.html as one JSON-LD graph covering Organization, WebSite, Person, OfferCatalog, Service, and WebPage entities.
